Instructions

  • The first question will be displayed with a set of multiple-choice answers
  • Select an answer by clicking on one of the options
  • The app will highlight your selected answer
  • Click the "Next" button to go to the next question, or "Previous" to go back
  • You can navigate through all the questions and select your answers
  • Once you have completed all the questions, the "submit" button will appear
  • Click the "submit" button to see your score
  • The app will display the number of questions you answered correctly, the number of questions you answered incorrectly, and the total number of questions.
